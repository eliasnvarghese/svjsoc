WELCOME TO St. Stephen's Syriac Orthodox Congregation
At St. Thomas Church, 1921 Las Palmas Ave, San Jose CA

Welcome to St. Stephen's Syriac Orthodox Congregation San Jose, California, USA. We have submitted ourselves in the hands of God Almighty to use us as He wills, for the glorification of His name. We approach these plans with openness, humility and hope. Come with us to share the joy of being used by God.

The congregation was inaugurated and the first Holy Qurbono celebrated by the Patriarchal Vicar of the Malankara Archdiocese of the Syriac Orthodox Church in North America, HE Mor Titus Yeldho on October 18th 2018

The Holy Qurbono is conducted every other Sunday at St. Thomas Syriac Orthodox Church 1921 Las Plumas Ave, San Jose, CA 95133, San Jose, California at 8:00 am

Contact Priest: Fr PC Kuriakose at 954-907-7154 / Coordinators at 408-475-2149
